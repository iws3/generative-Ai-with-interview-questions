1- Model routing? Automatically sending queries to the right AI tool based on content.

2- Intent detection? Using keyword matching, ML classifiers, or rule-based logic.

3- RAG vs LLM decision? RAG for specific facts/knowledge, LLM for general conversation.

4- Risks of routing? Wrong routing, cascading failures, increased latency.

5- Logging & tracing? Track which route was chosen, response times, success rates.

6- Evaluation metrics? Response accuracy, user satisfaction, routing accuracy, latency.

7- Enterprise scaling? Load balancing, caching, circuit breakers, monitoring.
